At Freebrella, we generate revenue through innovative advertising on our umbrellas. Each umbrella serves as a moving billboard, capturing attention across the city. Brands can showcase their advertisements on our umbrellas, gaining visibility as people use them.

Our Founder

Freebrella was founded by Ankit Pushpakar, a visionary dedicated to promoting sustainability and community connection. With a background in Software Developer and Cyber Security Analyst, he recognized the need for an accessible solution to unexpected rain. Inspired by the sharing economy, Ankit created Freebrella to provide free umbrella rentals, encouraging resource sharing and reducing waste. Through this platform, he aims to empower individuals to stay dry and protected from sun rays while fostering a more eco-friendly lifestyle.

Our Mission

At Freebrella, our mission is to provide a hassle-free, sustainable solution for staying dry during unexpected rain. We strive to make quality umbrellas accessible to everyone, promoting a culture of sharing and reducing waste in our communities

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a world where umbrella rentals are the norm, empowering individuals to embrace the elements without the burden of ownership. We aim to expand our reach globally, fostering community connections and environmental consciousness through innovative sharing solutions.
