Policy for Renting an Umbrella

  • Security Deposit: You have to deposit a security amount of ₹299 to get an umbrella. This amount is fully refundable if the umbrella is returned within 24 hours in good condition.
  • Return Condition: The umbrella must be returned within 24 hours in good condition. Failure to return the umbrella in good condition will result in forfeiture of your security deposit.
